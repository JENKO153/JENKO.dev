Holy Scroll Support

Holy Scroll is a quieter scripture experience built around a personalised feed, a saved vault, account-backed preferences, and optional support tiers.

If you need help, use the information below.

What Holy Scroll Does

  • shows a personalised scripture feed based on your onboarding selections
  • lets you like and save verses into your personal vault
  • stores and syncs your preferences, likes, and vault items with iCloud when available
  • offers optional daily reminder presets
  • includes feed, vault, and account tabs for reading, saving, and managing your profile
  • offers optional weekly support subscriptions through Apple

Common Questions

Do I need an account to use Holy Scroll? Yes. Holy Scroll currently uses Sign in with Apple so your feed preferences, vault, and account data can be restored and synced.

Does Holy Scroll sync between devices? Yes. Likes, saved verses, and preferences are designed to sync through iCloud when you use the same Apple account and iCloud is available.

Can I use Holy Scroll offline? The bundled scripture content and local app state remain available on-device, but sign-in, syncing, and purchases depend on Apple services and may require internet access.

How do reminders work? Choose a reminder preset in the app and allow notifications when prompted. If notification permission is denied, reminders will not be delivered.

How do support subscriptions work? Support is optional and runs through Apple’s in-app purchase system. Weekly support tiers can be started or restored through the app.

Troubleshooting

  • If Sign in with Apple is not completing, check your Apple ID status and internet connection, then try again.
  • If your saved verses or preferences are not appearing on another device, make sure both devices are using the same Apple account and that iCloud is available.
  • If reminders are not arriving, confirm notification permission is enabled for Holy Scroll and that a reminder preset is active in the app.
  • If a support purchase is not showing, reopen the app and use the restore purchases flow so Apple entitlements can refresh.
