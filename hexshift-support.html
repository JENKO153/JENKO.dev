        <div class="stack" style="margin-bottom:48px;">


          <div class="card">

            <div class="card-pad">

              <h4 style="font-family:'Orbitron',monospace; font-size:1rem; letter-spacing:0.05em; margin-bottom:10px;">How do I rotate a group of hexes?</h4>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">Tap any hex to select it, then tap a neighbouring hex to rotate the group of three around the shared edge. Matching colours in clusters of three or more will shatter and score points.</p>

            </div>

          </div>


          <div class="card">

            <div class="card-pad">

              <h4 style="font-family:'Orbitron',monospace; font-size:1rem; letter-spacing:0.05em; margin-bottom:10px;">What are the special hex types?</h4>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Mystery</strong> — reveals a random colour when matched.<br>

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Locked</strong> — must be cleared by an adjacent match before it can move.<br>

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Bomb</strong> — clears adjacent cells if not matched in time.<br>

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Spreader</strong> — slowly converts neighbouring hexes to its colour.<br>

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Prism</strong> — tap to instantly convert the 6 surrounding cells to one colour and trigger a shatter.

              </p>

            </div>

          </div>


          <div class="card">

            <div class="card-pad">

              <h4 style="font-family:'Orbitron',monospace; font-size:1rem; letter-spacing:0.05em; margin-bottom:10px;">How does scoring work?</h4>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">Each shatter scores base points multiplied by your current streak. Chain shatters in a single move to build your streak multiplier and rack up big scores. Streaks reset if a move produces no match.</p>

            </div>

          </div>


          <div class="card">

            <div class="card-pad">

              <h4 style="font-family:'Orbitron',monospace; font-size:1rem; letter-spacing:0.05em; margin-bottom:10px;">What are the different game modes?</h4>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Classic</strong> — play until you run out of moves. Focus on high scores and Game Center leaderboards.<br>

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Timed</strong> — race the clock to score as high as possible before time runs out.<br>

                <strong style="color:#ffb44b;">Hardcore</strong> — no continues, no safety net. One run, one shot.

              </p>

            </div>

          </div>


          <div class="card">

            <div class="card-pad">

              <h4 style="font-family:'Orbitron',monospace; font-size:1rem; letter-spacing:0.05em; margin-bottom:10px;">How do continues work?</h4>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">When your run ends in Classic or Timed mode you can watch a rewarded ad to continue from where you left off. Up to 3 continues are available per run.</p>

            </div>

          </div>


          <div class="card">

            <div class="card-pad">

              <h4 style="font-family:'Orbitron',monospace; font-size:1rem; letter-spacing:0.05em; margin-bottom:10px;">My score didn't submit to Game Center. What happened?</h4>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your device Settings before starting a run. Scores are submitted at the end of each game — if you were offline at the time, reconnect to the internet and the score should sync on your next launch.</p>

            </div>

          </div>


          <div class="card">

            <div class="card-pad">

              <h4 style="font-family:'Orbitron',monospace; font-size:1rem; letter-spacing:0.05em; margin-bottom:10px;">How do I turn off sound or music?</h4>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">Tap the settings icon on the main menu to toggle sounds on or off. Your preference is saved between sessions.</p>

            </div>

          </div>

              <p class="muted" style="line-height:1.85;">Yes — HexShift is fully supported on iPad with layout scaling to fit the larger screen.</p>
