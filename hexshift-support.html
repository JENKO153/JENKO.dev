FAQ

How do I rotate a group of hexes?

Tap any hex to select it, then tap a neighbouring hex to rotate the group of three around the shared edge. Matching colours in clusters of three or more will shatter and score points.

What are the special hex types?

Mystery — reveals a random colour when matched.
Locked — must be cleared by an adjacent match before it can move.
Bomb — clears adjacent cells if not matched in time.
Spreader — slowly converts neighbouring hexes to its colour.
Prism — tap to instantly convert the 6 surrounding cells to one colour and trigger a shatter.

How does scoring work?

Each shatter scores base points multiplied by your current streak. Chain shatters in a single move to build your streak multiplier and rack up big scores. Streaks reset if a move produces no match.

What are the different game modes?

Classic — play until you run out of moves. Focus on high scores and Game Center leaderboards.
Timed — race the clock to score as high as possible before time runs out.
Hardcore — no continues, no safety net. One run, one shot.

How do continues work?

When your run ends in Classic or Timed mode you can watch a rewarded ad to continue from where you left off. Up to 3 continues are available per run.

My score didn't submit to Game Center. What happened?

Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your device Settings before starting a run. Scores are submitted at the end of each game — if you were offline at the time, reconnect to the internet and the score should sync on your next launch.

How do I turn off sound or music?

Tap the settings icon on the main menu to toggle sounds on or off. Your preference is saved between sessions.

Does HexShift work on iPad?

Yes — HexShift is fully supported on iPad with layout scaling to fit the larger screen.
