Picket List Support

Picket List is a local-first roster tool built to create, edit, and share military-style picket lists quickly and simply.

If you need help, use the information below.

What Picket List Does

  • creates single staggered and double staggered picket lists
  • lets you assign or edit names for each slot
  • stores your most recent roster locally on your device
  • allows you to copy or share the generated picket list as a message
  • works without an internet connection

Common Questions

Does Picket List require internet? No. Picket List is designed to work fully on-device.

Does Picket List save my data? Yes. The app saves your most recent working roster locally on your device so you can reopen it and continue editing.

Does Picket List collect my personal information? No. Picket List does not collect, track, or upload your entered data.

Can I share the roster with others? Yes. You can copy or share the generated picket list as a message for group chats or handover use.

How does double staggered work? Double staggered is set up so the first two members start together, and the first member’s duty is split between the beginning and end of the overall window.

Troubleshooting

  • If the roster looks out of date, tap ISSUE or REISSUE to regenerate it from the current setup.
  • If the keyboard stays open, tap outside the input field or scroll the page to dismiss it.
  • If a saved roster looks incorrect, review the start time, end time, stagger mode, and personnel list, then regenerate the picket.
