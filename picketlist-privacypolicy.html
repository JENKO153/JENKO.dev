PICKET LIST

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

Overview

Picket List respects your privacy. This app is designed to work locally on your device and does not require an internet connection to create or manage picket lists.

Information We Collect

Picket List does not collect, track, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

All information entered into the app, including names, roster details, handover notes, challenges, and night orders, is stored locally on your device for app functionality only.

How Your Data Is Used

The information you enter is used only to:

create and edit picket lists

save your last used roster and notes on your device

allow you to export or copy roster text manually when you choose

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. Picket List does not upload your data to external servers or cloud services.

Third-Party Services

Picket List does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or external data collection services.

Data Sharing

Picket List does not share your information with the developer or any third party. If you choose to copy or share a generated message, that action is initiated entirely by you.

Children’s Privacy

Picket List does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, protection of that data depends in part on your device’s own security settings and access controls.

Your Choices

You can remove app data at any time by deleting the app from your device or clearing the app’s stored data through device-level controls if available.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted at this same location with a revised effective date.
